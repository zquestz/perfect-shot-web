Data Deletion

If you would like to delete the personal data associated with your Perfect Shot account while keeping your account active, you may do so by following the instructions below. This process removes your stored personal data but preserves your account so you can continue using the Service.

If you would like to delete your entire account and all associated data permanently, please visit our Account Deletion page instead.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us at contact@perfectshot.io. Place "Delete My Data" in the subject line and include your first name, last name, and e-mail address in the body of the e-mail. We will respond to your request within the time period stated by applicable law.

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, the following will be removed:

  • Gameplay progress, scores, and statistics;
  • Your username and profile information;
  • Any other personal data associated with your account.

Your account credentials will be retained so you can continue to sign in. Please note that certain information may also be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or compliance with legal obligations. We may request that you provide us with information necessary to confirm your identity before processing your request.

After your data is deleted, your gameplay progress and statistics will be reset. If you continue using the Service, new data will be collected as described in our Privacy Policy.
