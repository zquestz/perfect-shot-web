Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Perfect Shot Developers ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Perfect Shot mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve the Service:

  • Authentication data — When you sign in using Google Play Sign-In, Telegram Login, or Farcaster, we receive basic profile information (such as your account or user ID) required to create and manage your account.
  • Username — The display name you choose for your in-game profile.
  • Gameplay statistics — Scores, progress, and other gameplay data associated with your account.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — Technical information about errors and crashes to help us identify and fix issues.

We do not collect personal information beyond what is required for authentication and profile creation.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account;
  • Save and display your gameplay progress and statistics;
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the stability of the Service.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Sign-In — Used for authentication on Android. Google's privacy policy applies to the data they process. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Telegram Login — Used for authentication on Telegram. Telegram's privacy policy applies to the data they process. You can review it at telegram.org/privacy.
  • Farcaster — Used for authentication on the Farcaster platform. Farcaster's privacy policy applies to the data they process. You can review it at farcaster.xyz/~/privacy-policy.
  • Sentry — Used for error tracking and crash reporting. Only diagnostic and technical data is shared with Sentry. You can review their privacy policy at sentry.io/privacy.

We do not share any other data with third parties. We do not sell your information to anyone.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your data or account, we will remove it promptly, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on our servers located in the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your data or your account;
  • Revoke your consent for data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please visit our Data Deletion or Account Deletion pages, or contact us directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
