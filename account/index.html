Account Deletion

If you would like to permanently delete your Perfect Shot account and all associated data, you may do so by following the instructions below. Please note that account deletion is irreversible — once your account is deleted, all of your data, including gameplay progress, scores, and any other information associated with your account, will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, contact us at contact@perfectshot.io. Place "Delete My Account" in the subject line and include your first name, last name, and e-mail address in the body of the e-mail. We will respond to your request within the time period stated by applicable law.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and credentials;
  • Gameplay progress, scores, and statistics;
  • Any other personal data associated with your account.

Please note that certain information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or compliance with legal obligations. We may request that you provide us with information necessary to confirm your identity before processing your request.

If you only wish to delete your data while keeping your account, please visit our Data Deletion page instead.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at contact@perfectshot.io.
